About Republic City

Republic City is the capital of the United Republic of Nations. It is located on the southern shore of the Earth Kingdom. It was founded by Firelord Zuko and Avatar Aang after they defeated Zuko's father, Firelord Ozai, in 100 AG. It was build to be a city for refugees of the Hundred Year War.

The city is home to Yue Bay, a large city park, and skyscrapers. It is regarded as the most modern city in the world. Automobiles, motorcycles, and airships are frequently used here. The Metalbending Police Force, formed by Toph Beifong, uses metalbbending to enforce the law. Pro-bending is a popular sport within the city.

Republic City faced high levels of crime while Avatar Korra lived there, including the Anti-bending Revolution led by the Equalists, the assault of the Dark Avatar, and the invasion of the Earth Empire. The invasion of the Earth Empire resulted in a Spirit Gate opening in the middle of the city.
