About the Earth Kingdom

The Earth Kingdom is located in the eastern hemisphere of the world, and is the largest of all four nations. The Earth Kingdom is ruled by a king or queen. It has two prominent cities: Ba Sing Se and Omashu.

The Earth Kingdom was formed by humans who were gifted the power of energy bending by the Lion Turtles. However, some earth benders assumed a nomadic lifestyle, and stayed in one spot in what would be the Earth Kingdom for as long as they could. These people eventually formed a capital city, ruled by the peaceful spirit Tienhai. After Avatar Wan closed the spirit portals, the lion turtles rescinded their roles as protectors of humanity, and the people settled in the Earth Kingdom.

The different tribes and villages of the Earth Kingdom have constantly been at war since the Lion Turtles left, and only become one kingdom underneath the rule of an Earth King or Queen.
